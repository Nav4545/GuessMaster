About Me

I am Navraaj Chouhan, an 18-year-old currently living in Vancouver, British Columbia
I'm working toward a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from the University of the
Fraser Valley. My academic career is a consequence of a very deep love for technology
and computers, which actually sired the ideas this game explores. This is more of a project
than a pastime: it showcases what I love, be it innovation, problem-solving, or coding. It
lets me apply computer science in a way that truly matches my personal interests. As I go
on further into studies, I am motivated to know how technology will be a solution to enhance
our experiences with our passions and integrate the two worlds of academia and computers,
which fuel my creativity and ambition. I hope you enjoy this website I have created!
